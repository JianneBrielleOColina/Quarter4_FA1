Why Support Happy Animals Club?

Happy Animals Club is a non-profit animal rescue organization based in the Philippines. They focus on rescuing stray, abandoned, or abused animals and giving them a second chance. Here are some reasons why you should support them:

  1. Rescue and Rehabilitation: They save animals found in poor condition and provide them with shelter, food, and necessary medical treatments until they recover.
  2. Finds Loving Homes: Happy Animals Club helps animals find responsible and loving forever homes through their adoption program.
  3. Promotes Animal Welfare: They educate the community about responsible pet ownership and animal welfare while working closely with veterinarians and experts.
